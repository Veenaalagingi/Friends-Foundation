Our EVENTS

  • Reunion (24th November 2014)

About Reunion

On 24th November 2014, we all the students of SBSEM school(1990-91), batch assembled in our school premises on the auspicious occasion of our school founder Sri B Swamidoss sir birthday.We had a cake cut on that day and distributed to our school children and did Swatch Bharat in our school premises. We celebrated our BIG SIR birthday in the presence of Sarath Sir and Ramanaiah Sir.Almost 50+ classmates assembled in our school after 23 years of our X th standard.

  • Arundhathi committe school (2014)

About School

An anganvadi school at padugupadu village adapted by our friend's brother Mr.M Venkata Ramana,there we distributed clothes, books, sandals and food packets to the children of that school. And enjoyed the moments with the kids of that school along with our friends.

  • Buttermilk Distribution (2015)

About Buttermilk Distribution

During the hot summer of 2015, we Friends Foundation distributed around 500 litres of buttermilk per day to the patients coming to the famous Dr. RAMACHANDRA REDDY HOSPITALS, who treats the poor people at subsidized costs. Our efforts were praised by the doctors of the Hospital and made arrangements and helped us a lot. Looking for volunteers in Nellore to do such activities more.

  • Swachh Bharath (2014)

About Swachh Bharath

We did Swatcha Bharat in the school premises and got an accolade from the school neighbors. As part of it, we cleaned roads and canals surrounding to that school. And decided to help in the development of that school.

  • Chennai Floods Services (2015)

About Chennai Floods Services

Since inception of Friends Foundation the moto was to help the needy.When we heard about chennai floods the first thing came out mind was to send some emergency needs to the people who lost their homes in this situation.So we thought to collect and send the supplies with other supporters who were directly distributing at Chennai.All of sudden there was big donation given by one of our classmate Mr. Peruri Srinivas and Family which addded a big sum along with other donations made by our classmates.This made us directly to involve and go to Chennai and distribute at Manali.
