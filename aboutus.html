ABOUT FRIENDS Foundation

"Friends Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organization with a mission formed in 2014 by childhood school friends from Sri B swami doss English Medium High School batch of 1990-91 at Nellore,Andhra Pradesh,We are Proud to say that our friends had gathered after 24 years for a reunion and we all had a intention of CARE and GIVE back to society.That’s how FRIENDS FOUNDATION took its inception".

"Our motto is to provide better services and support to the needy unconditionally".

"Our foundation has also taught us to be united and it can always do better services to the society.Our foundation has also taught us to be united and it can always do better services to the society".
